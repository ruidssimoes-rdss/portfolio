Overview

F3 Finance is a custom financial dashboard that gives a small business owner complete visibility into their numbers. Income, expenses, P&L, flower inventory trends — all updated in real-time, all in one place.

Built specifically for The Floral Frenchie, it replaces manual spreadsheet tracking with automated reporting that actually gets used.

The Problem

Small business owners often fly blind when it comes to finances. They know roughly how much money is coming in and going out, but the details? Buried in bank statements and receipts. By the time they know they're losing money on something, it's too late.

The Floral Frenchie needed real-time visibility — not quarterly reports from an accountant, but daily insight into what's working and what isn't.

The Solution

F3 Finance pulls in transaction data and categorizes it automatically. The dashboard shows today's numbers, this week's trends, and this month's trajectory — all at a glance. No accounting degree required.

Special attention went into flower-specific metrics: which arrangements are most profitable, which flowers have the best margin, seasonal trend analysis. Data that actually matters for this specific business.

Key Features

  • Real-time P&L — Live profit and loss statement that updates as transactions come in.
  • Spend Analysis — Categorized expense breakdown with trend visualization and alerts.
  • Flower Trend Analysis — Track which products are most profitable and identify seasonal patterns.
  • Invoice Management — Generate and track invoices directly from the dashboard.

Results

The business owner now checks the dashboard daily — it's become part of the morning routine alongside coffee. Decisions that used to be gut feelings are now backed by data.

Within the first month, the dashboard helped identify an underperforming product line that was quietly losing money. That insight alone paid for the development time.
