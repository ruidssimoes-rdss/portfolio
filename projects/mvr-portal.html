Overview

MVR Portal is a custom-built internal tool for a security and construction company. It replaces a chaotic mix of spreadsheets and paper with a unified system for managing quotes, invoices, clients, and job tracking.

The portal is now used daily by the business, handling all their operational workflow from initial quote to final invoice.

The Problem

The business was drowning in paperwork. Quotes were created in Word, prices tracked in Excel, invoices generated manually. Finding information meant digging through folders. Nothing talked to anything else.

They needed a single source of truth — something simple enough to use without training, but powerful enough to handle their entire workflow.

The Solution

I built a custom web application that handles the complete business cycle. Create a quote, convert it to a job, track progress, generate an invoice — all in one place. Prices update automatically. Client history is always available. Nothing gets lost.

The interface was designed for people who aren't tech-savvy. Big buttons, clear labels, no hidden menus. If you can use a smartphone, you can use this portal.

Key Features

  • Quote Builder — Create professional quotes with automatic price calculation from a managed price list.
  • Invoice Generation — One-click conversion from completed jobs to professional PDF invoices.
  • Client Management — Complete history of every client interaction, quote, and job in one place.
  • Price Management — Centralized price list that automatically updates all quotes and templates.

Results

The portal has been in daily use since launch. Quote creation time dropped from 30+ minutes to under 5. Invoicing errors have essentially disappeared. The client can now track every aspect of their business from a single dashboard.

Most importantly, they actually use it — the ultimate measure of success for any internal tool.
