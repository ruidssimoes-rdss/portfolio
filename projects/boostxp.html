Overview

BoostXP was born from a simple observation: traditional fitness apps focus on tracking, but they miss the motivation. I wanted to build something that made working out feel like leveling up in a video game.

The app features a custom "Foresight Engine" that predicts your progress and adapts workouts accordingly, wrapped in a sleek dark UI with the signature "Midnight Lime" design system.

The Problem

Most fitness apps are glorified spreadsheets. They track your reps and sets, but they don't give you that dopamine hit that keeps you coming back. Gamification in fitness often feels gimmicky — badges that mean nothing, streaks that punish you for rest days.

I wanted to create something that felt genuinely rewarding without being manipulative.

The Solution

BoostXP treats your fitness journey like an RPG character build. Every workout earns XP. Every milestone unlocks new features. Your "stats" (strength, endurance, flexibility) grow based on what you train.

The Foresight Engine analyzes your patterns and suggests optimal workout times, predicts plateaus before they happen, and adjusts difficulty to keep you in the "flow zone" — challenged but not overwhelmed.

Key Features

  • XP & Leveling System — Earn experience points for every workout, level up your character, and unlock new features as you progress.
  • Foresight Engine — AI-powered predictions that adapt to your schedule, energy levels, and goals.
  • Stat Tracking — Visual progression of your strength, endurance, flexibility, and overall fitness level.
  • Custom Workout Builder — Create and save your own routines, or use AI-generated workouts based on your goals.

What I Learned

Building BoostXP taught me that the hardest part of app development isn't the code — it's the design decisions. Every feature had to earn its place. I cut more features than I shipped.

I also learned the value of building in public. Sharing progress on the app led to valuable feedback that shaped the final product in ways I couldn't have anticipated.
